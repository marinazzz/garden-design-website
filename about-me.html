ABOUT

Boris Dordevic

Boris Dordevic is a multi-awarded garden designer with Master Landscape Architect degree. Having a long experience in garden maintenance, garden building, garden designing and leading small and large garden project builds in the past, makes him to stand out from other garden designers. His unique background, knowledge and wide experience in garden industry will provide the best suited garden design on the market.

Mr. Boris beside Garden Design solution will consider maintenance, buildability, budget frame, irrigation, lights and how the designed garden will look in long run. Your investment in garden is top priority for Mr. Boris and he knows how important is to meet deadlines and be on the budget. Garden Design proposals are easy to read and understand because Mr. Boris using cutting edge professional softwares (AutoCAD, Lumion, ArchiCAD) to create precise technical drawings and realistic 3D illustration of the designed space.

In the past several years Mr. Boris specializes in Garden Design for private clients internationally and nationally. His portfolio contains over 80 garden designs from small country side homes to urban homes, balcony gardens, roof gardens, green walls and luxury homes. He is currently working on garden design projects for clients in Oslo, Viken and Akershus, but can consider clients from other parts of Norway!
