Garden design services overview

Garden Design

Garden Design is my top service. I am very proud to say that I offer the most complete garden design service in Norway! Choosing this service will give you the greatest value for your money!

Polite notice: due to the large number of inquiries, please be patient! I will reply to you soon as possible! From my experience it is the best to send me an inquiry on button below and take a place in a que. Thank you!

Garden Design includes:

  • 4 Consultations;
  • Initial Two Concept Designs;
  • Preliminary garden design;
  • Garden Master Plan;
  • Planting Plan, Planting List and Specifications;
  • Garden Lightning Plan and Specification;
  • Garden Irrigation Plan and Specification;
  • Material Plan and Specification;
  • VR 360o Panorama View of New Designed Space;
  • 3D Illustrations, CGI Images and 3D Walkthrough.

This offer is perfect for those who wants to build a new garden or to rebuild an existing garden.

Concept Garden Design

If you are looking to explore possibilities to upgrade or redesign your garden or you need inspirations to build your dream garden by yourself, I highly recommend Concept Design Service. You will get two concept garden ideas with included initial consultation.

You need more information? Please get in touch with me to arrange a meeting and we can discuss the best suited solution for your dream garden!

Garden Planning

You have drawn your dream garden design and now is time to build it? It could be smart to consult with me for Garden Planning before you start building your garden. In this way I can prevent possible major issues and conflicts during the build and save you time and money! Better safe than sorry!

I will closely check your design and layout and give you professional feedback.
